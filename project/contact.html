Frequently Asked Questions

When is the market open?

The market runs every Saturday from 8am to 2pm, May through October.

Are pets allowed?

Well-behaved, leashed pets are welcome. Please clean up after them.

Do you accept SNAP/EBT?

Yes! We accept SNAP/EBT and participate in the Double Up Food Bucks program.

Is there parking available?

Free parking is available in the Downtown Plaza parking garage with validation.
